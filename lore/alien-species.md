# Alien Species: The Resonant

## Overview

The native inhabitants of Kepler-442b do not fit conventional biological definitions. They are referred to by humans as **"The Resonant"**—a name derived from their primary mode of communication and existence.

## Biology

- **Form:** Non-corporeal consciousness distributed across crystalline neural networks
- **Medium:** Exists as patterns of electromagnetic and acoustic resonance within the planet's crystal structures
- **Individuality:** Unclear. The Resonant may be a single distributed intelligence or a collective of billions.
- **Perception:** Experiences time non-linearly. Can "remember" future events and "anticipate" past ones.

## Communication

The Resonant do not use sound or light in the traditional sense. They communicate through:

1. **Crystal Resonance:** Vibrating the planet's crystal structures at frequencies that directly stimulate neural tissue
2. **Electromagnetic Pulse:** Broad-spectrum EM bursts that carry encoded information
3. **Temporal Echo:** Projecting sensory experiences from different time periods simultaneously

## Relationship with Humans

The Resonant appear to view humanity as **children**—new, loud, destructive, but potentially teachable. Their response to human presence has been:

- **Observation:** Silent monitoring for 400 cycles
- **Testing:** Individual encounters with isolated humans (Fenwick, etc.)
- **Judgment:** The current phase. The Resonant are deciding whether humanity deserves to share their world.

## Known Manifestations

- **The Entity:** The primary interface form—smoke and light that adapts to human visual perception
- **The Spires:** Physical growths that serve as sensory organs and communication nodes
- **The Deep Song:** Planet-wide resonance pattern that may be the Resonant's "thought process"
