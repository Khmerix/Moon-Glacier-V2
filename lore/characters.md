# Characters

## Commander Elias Voss
- **Role:** Mission Commander, GL-7 Relief Transport
- **Background:** Three colony expeditions. Decorated veteran. Pragmatic to a fault.
- **Personality:** Protective of crew, suspicious of the unknown, values protocol
- **Arc:** Must choose between military duty and scientific wonder

## Dr. Elara Chen
- **Role:** Xenobiologist, Relief Transport Science Officer
- **Background:** Leading expert in extremophile biology. First to recognize Resonant biology.
- **Personality:** Curious, empathetic, willing to take risks for knowledge
- **Arc:** Science vs. survival. Can she study without destroying?

## Dr. Silas Fenwick
- **Role:** Former GL-7 Chief Geologist (Missing)
- **Background:** Descended into the crevasse 14 cycles ago. Presumed dead. Actually first human to make contact.
- **Status:** Changed. His consciousness may be partially integrated with the Resonant.

## The Entity
- **Role:** Primary Resonant Interface
- **Nature:** Not an individual but a manifestation of the collective consciousness
- **Motivation:** Testing humanity. Deciding if they are worthy of co-existence.
- **Voice:** Speaks in resonant frequencies that bypass ears and speak directly to neural tissue.

## The Player
- **Role:** You. A specialist on the relief transport.
- **Background:** Undefined. Your choices define who you become.
- **Arc:** The bridge between two species. Or the spark that burns everything.
