> **NARRATION**
> The dais responds to your weight. Not with sound—with light. Every crystal in the chamber brightens simultaneously, their internal pulses synchronizing into a rhythm.

> **NARRATION**
> A beat. A heartbeat. A billion years old and suddenly awake.

> **NARRATION**
> The air shimmers. Not heat—something else. A distortion in the visible spectrum, like looking through water.

> **NARRATION**
> And then it coalesces. Smoke made of light. Light made of intention. A figure that is not a figure, existing in the space between the crystals, neither solid nor ethereal but somehow both.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *ancient*
> "You are not the first to wake us. You are not the first to burn. Will you be the first to listen?"

> **NARRATION**
> The voice doesn't come through your radio. It resonates in your chest cavity, in your bones, in the calcium of your teeth. It speaks in frequencies that bypass your ears entirely.

> **DIALOGUE: Dr. Elara Chen** *terrified*
> "It's not using sound. It's using resonance. The crystals are vibrating at frequencies that stimulate our nervous systems directly. It's... it's inside our heads."

> **NARRATION**
> The entity shifts. For a moment, it looks almost human. A face you almost recognize. Then it dissolves back into iridescent smoke.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *curious*
> "Your kind arrives with fire and hunger. Will you be different?"
