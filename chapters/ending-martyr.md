> **NARRATION**
> Kael caught Marcus at the extraction chamber. Marcus had already begun the protocol—waking the entity with Helix frequencies, forcing it into consciousness before it was ready.

> **NARRATION**
> The entity screamed. The moon shook. And Kael understood: if the protocol completed, the entity would lash out. Not at Marcus. At everything.

> **NARRATION**
> He didn't hesitate. He shot the control console, overloaded the power grid, and triggered the emergency collapse. The ceiling came down in tons of ice and steel.

> **DIALOGUE: Marcus Thorne** *buried*
> "Kael! You fool! You could have had everything!"

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *smiling*
> "I did."

> **NARRATION**
> The team escaped. Kael did not. When they dug through the rubble three days later, they found his body wrapped around the shattered console, hand still on the overload switch.

> **NARRATION**
> The entity was saved. Humanity was saved. And Commander Kael Vance became the first human buried on a moon that was no longer just ice.
