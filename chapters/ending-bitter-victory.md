> **NARRATION**
> They understood each other, Kael and the entity. Two beings who had lost too much to ever be whole again.

> **NARRATION**
> The entity taught them what it could. Not everything—some knowledge was too dangerous, some memories too painful. But enough. Enough to change humanity's trajectory. Enough to ensure that Sera's death had meaning.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *gentle*
> "She would be proud of you, Commander. The choice you made. The pain you carried without letting it break you."

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *grieving*
> "Pride doesn't bring her back."

> **NARRATION**
> "No. But it honors her. And honor is the only immortality the dead can receive."

> **NARRATION**
> The team left GL-7 on the anniversary of Sera's death. Kael scattered her ashes across the glacier she had died exploring.

> **NARRATION**
> The ice took them. The moon held them. And somewhere, in the spaces between atoms, Sera Voss drew stars for a daughter she would never see grow up.

> **NARRATION**
> But the stars were brighter now. Because she had touched them.
