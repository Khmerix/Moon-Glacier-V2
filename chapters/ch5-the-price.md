> **NARRATION**
> The entity's form flickered when Kael asked about the previous civilization. For a moment, it looked almost afraid.

> **NARRATION**
> "They were like you," it said. "Curious. Hungry. Beautiful. We taught them everything. How to weave starlight into matter. How to speak across galaxies. How to live forever."

> **NARRATION**
> The chamber darkened. The crystals turned red.

> **NARRATION**
> "And when they had learned everything, they decided they did not need teachers anymore. They built weapons to kill gods. They nearly succeeded."

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *ancient*
> "We did not destroy them, Commander. We saved them. Every mind, every memory, every dream—they live here, in the ice. Forever. Is that not mercy?"

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *horrified*
> "You didn't save them. You consumed them. This whole moon is a graveyard, and you're the gravedigger."

> **NARRATION**
> The entity turned to Kael. "Your kind will do the same. It is the pattern. The only question is whether you will be the exception. Or the next memorial."
