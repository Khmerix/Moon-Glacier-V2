> **NARRATION**
> Kael walked into the entity's chamber with Sera's blood still on his hands. He didn't raise his weapon. He didn't speak. He just stood there, empty, waiting for the moon to explain why it had taken her.

> **NARRATION**
> The entity appeared. Smaller this time. Almost respectful.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *sorrowful*
> "Your kind dies so easily. A fall. A breath. A moment of stillness. We did not take her, Commander. She was borrowed, and her time ended."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael laughed. It was not a happy sound. "Borrowed. Like a library book."

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *curious*
> "You grieve deeply for one. Yet your species kills millions without mourning. Why is one death a tragedy and a million a statistic?"

> **NARRATION**
> Kael had no answer. He only had rage, and grief, and the hollow place where Sera used to be.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *broken*
> "Because I knew her name. Because she drew stars for her daughter. Because she deserved to go home."
