> **NARRATION**
> Kael stood before the United Nations General Assembly and told them everything. The entity. The previous civilization. The danger. The opportunity.

> **NARRATION**
> Half the chamber called him a madman. Half called him a prophet. One nation—one small, ambitious nation—called him an asset.

> **DIALOGUE: UN Secretary-General** *grave*
> "Commander Vance, you're asking us to abandon a moon that could hold the key to faster-than-light travel. On the word of a dead expedition's warning?"

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *steadfast*
> "I'm asking you to be worthy of the stars before you reach them."

> **NARRATION**
> The vote was close. But it passed. GL-7 was declared a protected quarantine zone. No ships. No expeditions. No corporate mining rights.

> **NARRATION**
> Kael went home to a small apartment and a smaller pension. He was not famous. He was not rich. But when he looked up at the night sky, he knew he had done one thing right.

> **NARRATION**
> He had told the truth. And for once, someone listened.
