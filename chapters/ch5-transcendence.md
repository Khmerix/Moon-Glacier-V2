> **NARRATION**
> Elara opened the communication channel. Not with words—with thought. The crystals hummed, and suddenly the entire team was inside the same mind.

> **NARRATION**
> They saw the entity's memories. Not filtered, not translated. Raw. The birth of stars. The death of galaxies. The loneliness of being the last thing alive in a universe that had grown cold.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *transcendent*
> "It's not an invasion, Kael. It's an adoption. It wants us to become its children. To carry its memories forward. We would live forever. We would see the universe end."

> **DIALOGUE: Nyx Oriel** *afraid*
> "But would we still be us? Or just... echoes?"

> **NARRATION**
> The entity offered its hand. Not physically—conceptually. A choice to step outside the cage of individual consciousness and become part of something eternal.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *awestruck*
> "What happens if we say yes?"

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *gentle*
> "You become the song. And the song never ends."
