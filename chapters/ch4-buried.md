> **NARRATION**
> The emergency tunnel collapsed twelve meters in. Sera was caught in the secondary cave-in. By the time Kael dug her out, she wasn't breathing.

> **NARRATION**
> He performed CPR for nine minutes. Nine minutes of breaking ribs, of forcing air into lungs full of ice dust, of screaming her name until his throat bled.

> **NARRATION**
> She didn't wake up.

> **DIALOGUE: Nyx Oriel** *broken*
> "Sera... no. No, no, no. She was supposed to make it home. She has a daughter. She has a little girl who draws her pictures of stars."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael closed Sera's eyes. His hands shook. Not from cold. From rage.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *weeping*
> "The moon took her. It knew we were here, and it took her. This isn't random, Kael. This is personal."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael stood. He looked at the collapsed tunnel, at Sera's body, at the team that was no longer whole. Then he looked deeper. Where the ice was thinnest.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *empty*
> "We finish the mission. For Sera. We find whatever did this, and we make it understand what it cost."
