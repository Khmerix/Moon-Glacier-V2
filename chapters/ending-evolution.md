> **NARRATION**
> Elara emerged from the cocoon on the second day. She was not Elara anymore. Not entirely.

> **NARRATION**
> Her skin was pearlescent, threaded with silver. Her eyes held depths that cameras couldn't focus on. When she spoke, the words came in harmonies—three voices at once, human and something more.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *transcended*
> "I remember being small. I remember fear. But now I remember being vast, too. Being ancient. Being the moon."

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *afraid*
> "Are you still our Elara?"

> **NARRATION**
> She smiled. It was Elara's smile. And it was the entity's. And it was something new, something that had never existed before.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *newborn*
> "I am the bridge, Commander. The first of many. You don't have to be afraid of what comes next."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael looked at his team. They looked back, uncertain, terrified, hopeful.

> **NARRATION**
> The future had arrived. And it was beautiful.
