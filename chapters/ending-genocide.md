> **NARRATION**
> Kael didn't run. He unloaded every weapon he had into the breach, then jury-rigged the transport's reactor into a bomb and rolled it into the darkness.

> **NARRATION**
> The explosion was visible from orbit. A flower of white fire blooming across the glacier, turning ancient ice into steam, ancient flesh into ash.

> **NARRATION**
> The entity screamed. The moon screamed. And then, finally, there was silence.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *burnt*
> "It's done."

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *weeping*
> "We killed something older than life on Earth. Something that only wanted to talk. And we burned it because we were afraid."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael looked at his hands. They were blistered, blackened, shaking. But they were human hands. Human, frightened, violent hands.

> **NARRATION**
> He had saved humanity from the unknown. He wondered, as the transport climbed toward the stars, if he had also saved humanity from its own future.
