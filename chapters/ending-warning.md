> **NARRATION**
> The entity taught them. Cautiously. Slowly. Every lesson came with a history lecture, a cautionary tale, a reminder of what happened to those who learned too fast and forgot to listen.

> **NARRATION**
> Kael absorbed it all. The physics. The philosophy. The terrible, beautiful truth that the universe was full of minds like the entity's—ancient, patient, waiting for species to grow up enough to join the conversation.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *hopeful*
> "You are not the first we have taught. You are not the tenth. But you may be the first to remember the warning."

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *humble*
> "What is the warning?"

> **NARRATION**
> The entity's form dimmed. "Power without wisdom is extinction. Knowledge without compassion is a prison. And the universe has no patience for children who play with fire."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael returned to Earth six months later. Not with weapons. Not with technology. With stories.

> **NARRATION**
> And for the first time in human history, the most powerful thing a commander brought back from the stars was a lesson in listening.
