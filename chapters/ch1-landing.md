> **NARRATION**
> The transport shudders as it breaches the upper atmosphere. Below, the glacier fields of Kepler-442b stretch to every horizon—an endless expanse of blue-white ice shot through with veins of luminescent mineral deposits.

> **NARRATION**
> Your hands grip the armrests. The viewport is a frame of impossible beauty: frozen lightning captured in ice, ancient pressure ridges forming mountain ranges of crystallized methane, and far below—a single scar of black against the white.

> **NARRATION**
> The outpost. GL-7. Humanity's farthest toehold in the Kepler sector.

> **DIALOGUE: Commander Voss** *urgent*
> "Brace for descent! The magnetic interference is stronger than our readings suggested. I'm losing altitude control—"

> **NARRATION**
> The ship lurches. Warning klaxons fill the cabin. Through the viewport, the mining outpost grows larger—a cluster of black spires against the ice, emergency beacons pulsing crimson.

> **DIALOGUE: Commander Voss** *gritted*
> "Something's wrong down there. Those aren't docking lights. Those are emergency strobes. The outpost is in distress."

> **NARRATION**
> You have seconds to decide. The transport is coming in hot, and Voss's hands are white on the controls.

## Choices

- **Touch down at the main landing pad** → ch2-outpost-main
  *Effects: courage +1, vossTrust +1*
  *Flavor: Standard protocol. Standard risks. The pad is closest to the command center.*

- **Divert to the emergency beacon** → ch2-outpost-beacon
  *Effects: courage +2, vossTrust -1*
  *Flavor: The beacon burns crimson at the eastern ridge. Whatever happened, started there.*

- **Scan the glacier fields first** → ch2-outpost-beacon
  *Effects: tech +2*
  *Flavor: Information before action. The transport's sensors can paint a thermal picture in three seconds.*

- **Bail out and glide to the outpost** → ch2-outpost-main
  *Effects: courage +3, vossTrust +2*
  *Condition: stat courage >= 0*
  *Flavor: Insane. But the transport is a death trap if it crashes with fuel reserves intact.*
