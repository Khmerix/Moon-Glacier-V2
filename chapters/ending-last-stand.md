> **NARRATION**
> Kael didn't negotiate. He didn't surrender. He turned the outpost's mining lasers outward, jury-rigged the reactor into a directional pulse weapon, and made his stand.

> **NARRATION**
> The first drop-ship disintegrated in a halo of superheated plasma. The second tried to flank and met Nyx's improvised mines—ice packed with thermite, simple and devastating.

> **NARRATION**
> Helix Corp had never fought a war. They had conducted acquisitions. This was different. This was a species defending its right to meet the universe on its own terms.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *defiant*
> "You want the entity? Come through us. Every. Single. One."

> **NARRATION**
> The third ship retreated. And the fourth. By dawn, the glacier belonged to the outpost again.

> **NARRATION**
> Kael stood on the ice, smoking rifle in hand, and watched Helix abandon the sky. Behind him, the entity's spires pulsed with new light.

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *respectful*
> "Your kind fights well for something so fragile. Perhaps you are worth teaching after all."
