> **NARRATION**
> Elara entered quarantine willingly. She helped design the protocols, ran her own tests, and documented every stage of the transformation with a scientist's detached precision.

> **NARRATION**
> By hour twelve, her skin had begun to luminesce. By hour twenty, she no longer needed to speak—her thoughts leaked into the team's minds like warmth from a fire.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *fading*
> "I'm still here, Kael. But I'm also... there. In the ice. In the song. It's not painful. It's vast."

> **NARRATION**
> By hour thirty-six, the transformation was complete. Elara's body dissolved into crystal, merging with the moon's architecture. Her consciousness joined the entity's chorus.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *kneeling*
> "Will she remember us?"

> **DIALOGUE: The Entity** *gentle*
> "She will remember everything. She will be everything. And she will wait for you."

> **NARRATION**
> The quarantine lab was sealed forever. But on cold nights, the team swore they could hear Elara's laughter in the wind.
