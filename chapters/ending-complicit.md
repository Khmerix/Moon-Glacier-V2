> **NARRATION**
> Kael destroyed the transmitter. Wiped the logs. Told Earth that GL-7 was barren, that the expedition had died in a storm, that there was nothing worth finding.

> **NARRATION**
> Six months later, Helix Corp offered him a position. Vice President of Xenological Acquisition. Seven figures. A penthouse overlooking the Pacific. A life of silence bought and paid for.

> **DIALOGUE: Helix Director** *toasting*
> "To Commander Vance. The man who knew when to look away."

> **NARRATION**
> Kael raised his glass. The wine was excellent. The view was breathtaking. And every night, when he closed his eyes, he heard the entity singing in the dark.

> **NARRATION**
> Not accusing. Not angry. Just singing. Waiting. Knowing that someday, someone else would descend. Someone who wouldn't look away.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *alone*
> "I'm sorry. I'm so sorry."

> **NARRATION**
> The penthouse was warm. The glacier was far away. And Kael Vance had never been colder.
