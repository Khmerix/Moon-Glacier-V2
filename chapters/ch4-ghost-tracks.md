> **NARRATION**
> They found the bodies at dawn. Six of them, the previous expedition, frozen in a circle around a transmitter. They had died holding hands.

> **NARRATION**
> Elara checked the transmitter. It was still broadcasting. A single message, looping, in fifty languages, dead and living.

> **DIALOGUE: Elara Synn** *haunted*
> "Listen. 'We opened the door. We welcomed it in. It offered knowledge, and we drank until we were drunk on starlight. Then it asked for payment. We are the payment.'"

> **NARRATION**
> The bodies weren't just frozen. They were transformed. Crystalline growths extruded from their skin, merging them with the ice. They had become part of the moon.

> **DIALOGUE: Sera Voss** *sickened*
> "They didn't die. Look at the tissue integration. They're still alive. In there. Somewhere."

> **NARRATION**
> One of them moved. Just an eye, opening, silver and ancient. It looked at Kael. And it spoke with six voices at once.

> **DIALOGUE: Frozen Expedition** *echoing*
> "Run, little commander. Run and tell them what waits in the dark. Or stay, and join the chorus."
