> **NARRATION**
> They merged on the winter solstice. All five of them—Kael, Elara, Marcus, Nyx, Sera—joining their consciousness with the entity in a single, blinding moment of connection.

> **NARRATION**
> Kael felt Elara's wonder. He felt Marcus's guilt. He felt Nyx's freedom, and Sera's compassion, and the entity's ancient, aching loneliness.

> **NARRATION**
> And then he felt himself dissolve. Not into death. Intowe. Into us. Into a being that remembered being human but was no longer limited by human boundaries.
