> **NARRATION**
> Kael took the technology by force. The entity resisted, but Kael had brought explosives. Enough to crack the central crystal. Enough to make the entity choose between death and compliance.

> **NARRATION**
> It chose compliance. The spires dimmed. The song became a whisper. And Kael Vance became the most powerful human who had ever lived.

> **NARRATION**
> Within a year, Earth bowed. Within five, the solar system. Within ten, Kael's fleets reached the neighboring stars, armed with weapons the entity had never intended for war.

> **DIALOGUE: Kael Vance** *crowned*
> "I did what had to be done. Peace is a luxury for the weak."

> **NARRATION**
> The entity watched from its prison of shattered crystal. It had seen this before. It would see it again. And when Kael's empire finally fell—when hubris met the cold mathematics of entropy—the entity would still be waiting.

> **NARRATION**
> Patient. Ancient. Unforgiving.
